About EDM Sound System

EDM Sound System is a radio show hosted on WREK in Atlanta, bringing you the best of local DJ's and producers, every Saturday night from 9:00 PM - 12:00 AM ET.
Tune into EDM Sound System for:

  • A wide variety of music spanning the entire EDM spectrum
  • The hottest mixes from the local scene
  • Exclusive interviews with hit artists
  • Ticket give-aways for upcoming shows
  • And more!

If you're in the Atlanta area, you can tune in on 91.1 FM. You can also listen to the show right here on this very website! During the show, the player will automatically connect to the online live stream, courtesy of WREK.
